# l10n_uk - United Kingdom Accounting

## Overview
- **Name:** United Kingdom - Accounting
- **Country:** United Kingdom (GB)
- **Category:** Accounting/Localizations/Account Charts
- **Version:** 1.1
- **Author:** SmartMode LTD
- **License:** LGPL-3
- **Dependencies:** `account`, `base_iban`, `base_vat`
- **Auto-installs:** `account`

## Description
UK accounting localization providing:
- CT600-ready chart of accounts
- VAT100-ready tax structure
- UK counties listing (InfoLogic)
- Additional adaptations for UK reporting

## Models
No Python models. Fully data-driven.

## Data Files
- `data/country_group.xml` — EU country grouping
- `data/l10n_uk_chart_data.xml` — UK chart of accounts
- `data/account_tax_report_data.xml` — VAT 100 report and CT600-compatible structure
- `demo/l10n_uk_demo.xml` / `demo/demo_company.xml` — Demo data
